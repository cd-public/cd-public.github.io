ADVISING

Updates:

Effective for students joining Willamette in 2025 or latter, there are now two CS degrees - a BA and a BS. The BS is intended to be (but not yet) accredited under ABET, a national organization accrediting degree programs in "Engineering and Technology". The BA was the existing major from years prior to 2025, and versus the BS lacks division coursework within the major and lacks supporting coursework in mathematics and science.
