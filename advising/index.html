ADVISING

Updates:

We expect to be able to offer a novel major in "Computer Science and Engineering" for Spring 2026 and latter graduates.

  • Interested students should contact Prof. Calvin Deutschbein (ckdeutschbein@willamette.edu) at earliest convenience.
  • Vs. "Computer Science", offers stronger scaffolding for dedicated computational career paths in industry and research, with less breadth outside of computing.
  • Major plan is still subject to university-level approval.
  • We expect to be able to achieve degree-specific accreditation with this proposed plan, vs. institutional (e.g. Willamette) accreditation for other degrees.
